Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Simply browse our services, select the one you want, and click the "Book Now" button. You'll need to create an account if you haven't already.

How are payments processed?

We use secure payment gateways to process all transactions. Your payment information is always protected.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking, but cancellation policies may vary depending on the service and timing.

How do I become an organizer?

Register as an organizer, complete your profile, and submit your services for review. Once approved, you can start accepting bookings.
